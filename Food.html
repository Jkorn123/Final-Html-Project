Jiro's resturant

Jiro Ono

Jiro's resturant or Sukiyabashi Jiro is a sushi resturant in Ginza, Chuo, Tokyo, Japan. It is owned and operated by sushi master Jiro Ono. It was the first sushi resturant to receive three michelin stars. An interesting fact about the resturant is that customers cannot not make reservations directly for seating, but have to make reservations through the concierge of a luxury hotel because of issues with customers not arriving on time.
