Video on Things To Do in Japan:


Mount Fuji:


Mount fuji is one of Japan's most majestic and recognizable landmarks. It is the country's highest mountain peak reaching over 3,776 meters high. Mount Fuji can even be seen from Tokyo over 100 kilometers away.



Hiroshima Memorial Park


These are some buildings which still stand from the August 1945 bombing of Hiroshima. The standing of this city commemorates the victims of the world's first nuclear assault. This memorial is visited by over millions every year. In fact, Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park lays at the very epicenter of the nuclear blast.



Imperial Palace


Imperial Palace is Japan's most famous landmark surrounded by moats and walls. However, the palace is off limits due to the fact that it is currnetly being occupied by the imperial family. There are many views of the palace from the very famous Nijubashi Bridge.
